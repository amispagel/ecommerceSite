Presentation

Mobile commerce is increasing, highlighting the need
to respond and utilize its growth with new
technology and innovation.
Mobile ecommerce is the use of mobile devices to make
purchases online.







Mobile Payments
E-Commerce consumers are now not only comfortable buying online, they're also allowing payment information to be stored within mobile applications. Consumers used to be afraid to use their phone to purchase goods, but new authentication methods such as Apple's fingerprint scanner and Google Wallet has created confidence in security.

Clicktale
Clicktale is one of the leaders in analytics, in business since 2006, and had grown by 5,553% between 2006 and 2011. It not only creates heat maps, but also creates filterable reports for its recordings.
“Define any business process, scenario or funnel to discover which visitors complete the process, which drop out, and why. Filter your visitors and watch video session playbacks of individual users that match your specific business needs. “

Mouseflow
Mouseflow shows actual video of what users are doing, as well as heat maps showing where user’s mouses are and where they click. They added statistics and page analysis in September 2014.

Packet Sniffing
With many websites running on mainly Javascript instead of Flash, making tracking scripts more of a burden on websites. Now, packet sniffing is becoming popular once again with built in support for AJAX, and localized software that runs on your server to record all data going in and out of your webserver/appserver. It does away with “complicated tagging logic to get content already on the page into a format suitable for sending back to the reporting tool.”

Auriq- Essentia
Log Management and Analysis Platform
- “With AuriQ Essentia, data analysis that once took weeks or days, can now be accomplished in hours. “
- Convert Apache logs into a readable format
- Redshift Integration is a scalable SQL service that allows users to create rules to scan and categorize their files, and is completely scriptable (meaning you can schedule scans in intervals)
- Perform simple queries without the need to move any database files

Payment Frameworks
